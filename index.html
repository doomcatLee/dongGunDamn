About Alex

My English name is Alex Lee, but my original name, Lee Dong-Gun (이동건) was given to me by a professional name maker in Korea where "Dong" stands for "East", and "Gun" stands for "build". One could interpret this as building a successful business in the East but despite the interpretation of the name, I have immigrated to the West of the west, here in Portland, OR.

I enjoy both product management and programming when it comes to development. I consider myself to be extremely competitive and passionate when it comes to things that interests me. As a result, I am always thinking about what product could be successful, and how I could implement it to grow successfully in the market.

In my spare time, I like to play and write music. Living without music is to live color blind in this world.
